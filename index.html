About the course

An 8‑week live cohort on practical, no‑code AI for research and productivity

Bootcamp format

Weekly live workshops and help sessions, community forums, personalized feedback, capstone coaching, and lifetime tutorial access.

Assessment

Quizzes (15%), assignments (35%), attendance/participation (10%), final project (40%). Passing grade: 80%.

Why it matters

Build real‑world AI workflows for writing, analysis, automation, and communication—without heavy coding.

Modules (at a glance)

1 · Websites, Documents & Presentations
LLM basics; generating Markdown/LaTeX/HTML; no‑code research sites; structured docs; AI for slide/poster design.
2 · AI‑Automated Data Analysis
Code Interpreter & Julius for fast analysis; automated dashboards; dataset transforms; batch file ops; automated reports & charts.
3 · GenAI for Research & Text Analysis
Literature reviews with Elicit/LiteRev; text classification; sentiment; knowledge maps; summarization pipelines; synthesis systems.
4 · Custom GPTs & Chatbots
Chat architecture, context windows & RAG; building custom GPTs; Botpress chatbots; connecting knowledge bases.
5 · AI for Task Automation
Agentic flows for repetitive work; email automations; browser‑control agents and orchestration.
6 · GenAI for Communication & Outreach
Multi‑audience summaries; figures & diagrams; video abstracts; prompts for visualization; content scheduling.
7 · Capstone Project & Showcase
Progress checks, peer feedback, final presentation, and public Q&A.
